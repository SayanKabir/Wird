# Privacy Policy for Wird

**Last Updated:** March 2, 2026

Welcome to **Wird**. Your privacy is critically important to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by Wird and how it is used.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

**Wird is an offline-first application.** 
We believe that your spiritual habits and personal data are your own business.

- **Location Data**: Wird requires access to your device's location (latitude and longitude) strictly to calculate accurate astronomical prayer times and determine the Qibla direction. **This data is processed and stored locally on your device.** We do not transmit, share, or store your location data on any external servers.
- **Habit Tracking & Statistics**: All data regarding your prayer logs, streaks, sunnah habits, tasbih counts, and overall progress are stored entirely locally on your device using an offline database (Hive).
- **Settings & Preferences**: Your app preferences (themes, notification settings, calculation methods) are kept locally.

## 2. Third-Party Services

Wird is designed to function cleanly without invasive third-party tracking. We do **not** use any analytics trackers, crash reporters, or advertising frameworks (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or AdMob).

We may use basic APIs to fetch necessary external public data:
- **Weather Services**: We may fetch generic weather data based on your coordinates to display on the dashboard. This request does not identify you or your device.

## 3. Data Security

Because all your data remains on your physical device, the security of your information is tied to the security of your device. We recommend utilizing your device's built-in security features (PIN, biometric lock, etc.) to protect your data.

## 4. Children's Information

We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Given that we collect no personal data to begin with and do not transmit data to servers, the app is entirely safe for users of all ages.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date written above and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will be in effect immediately after being posted.

If we update, amend or make any changes to our privacy policy, those changes will be posted here.
